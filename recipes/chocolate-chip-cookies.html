Chocolate Chip Cookies

A Classic Homemade Treat

Cookies! Loved the world over, this is a simple recipe for simply outstanding cookies.

These cookies are made with love. So make sure to only eat a few at a time!

This recipe doesn't stray too far from the original, but you can change some amounts to suit your tastes!

Here's a list of the ingredients:

  • 1 cup or 240 ml or g of softened butter
  • 1 cup or 240 ml or g of sugar
  • 1 cup or 240 ml of g of packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons or 8.4 g or 10 ml of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon or 4.2 g or 5 ml of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons or 8.4 g or 10 ml of warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon or 2.1 g or 5 ml of salt
  • 3 cups or 720 g of all purpose flour
  • 2 cups or 480 g of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • (optional) 1 cup or 240 g of nuts eg: walnuts or macadamia nuts

Here are the steps you'll need to follow:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F or 180 degrees C
  2. Beat the butter, sugar, and brown sugar in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs 1 at a time and then add the vanilla extract.
  4. Dissolve the baking soda into the warm water then add to the batter along with the salt.
  5. Stir in the flour and then the chocolate chips. Add the nuts here if you want them.
  6. Drop small spheres of dough on an ungreased baking sheet roughly 2 to 3 inches or 4 to 6 cm apart.
  7. Bake in the oven until the edges are browned. Usually this takes about 10 minutes.
  8. Cool the cookies.
  9. Store the cookies for later or eat!

Enjoy your cookies!

Please be careful about your caloric intake, and remember to enjoy the more savory dishes in moderation.
