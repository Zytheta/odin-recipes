Mac & Cheese

Creamy Cheesy Pasta!

Have you ever wanted an easy to make recipe using just the blue box plus a few extra ingredients? If so, keep reading and try some new interesting ideas to embellish and relish this iconic American pasta!

For this recipe we're just going to need a box of Kraft Mac & Cheese, some cream, and butter.

This is for sure a twist on the original which will recall those fond childhood memories of eating this quick and easy dish.

For those who want an extra kick, add a splash of your favorite hot sauce or a healthy grind of black pepper.

Here's a list of the ingredients:

  • A box of Kraft Mac & Cheese
  • 20 ml of cream
  • 4 tbsp or 25 g of butter
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • A dash of your favorite hot sauce

Here are the steps you'll need to follow:

  1. Bring 2 quarts or 1 liter of water in a pot to boil.
  2. Add the Mac & Cheese and boil for 7 minutes, stirring occassionaly.
  3. Drain the water and add the butter.
  4. Stir in the butter until the noodles are coated in a small layer of butter.
  5. Add the packet of cheese powder.
  6. Give 3 small stirs to spread out the powder, then add the cream.
  7. Stir and combine everything until incorporated.
  8. (Optional) Add some black pepper or hot sauce.

Enjoy your Mac & Cheese!

Please be careful about your sodium intake, and remember to enjoy the more savory dishes in moderation.
